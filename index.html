About ISDC'23

Intelligent systems refer broadly to computer embedded or controlled systems, machines and devices that posses a certain degree of intelligence with the capacity to gather and analyse data and communicate with other systems. There is a growing interest to developing intelligent technologies that enable users to accomplish complex tasts in different environments with relative ease.

What is Our Goal?

The First Internation Conference on Intelligent systems and Data Computing (ISDC'23) aims to bring together researchers in related fields to explore and discuss various aspects of intelligent systems and Data Computing applications. The Conference will provide excellent opportunities for the presentation of interesting new research resulta and discussion about them, leading to knowledge transfer and generation of new ideas.
